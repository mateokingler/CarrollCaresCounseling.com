Hi! I’m Anna Carroll

Licensed Professional Counselor in Kansas

Sometimes we don't feel connected to ourselves. Our excitement and motivation in life is just gone. Depression, anxiety, and all the "what if's" set in which become overwhelming. How do I get out of this rut? What can I do to feel better with myself and move forward in life? Why can't I be like others around me? These are hard questions and can feel intense. Let’s connect with your body and see where you are feeling lost or out of control. Let’s talk about being comfortable with yourself again.

About Me

I am a participant and advocate for intuitive eating and Healthy at Every Size (HAES) as well as a diet culture fighter. Our society has forced ideals about our weight showing how healthy we are when it is not true. Every size is beautiful and our appearance cannot tell you how amazing we and healthy we truly are. I have Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) and advocate for better education and better health for women and men who suffer from this syndrome. This syndrome has been misdiagnosed and mistreated for a long time and needs to have better education and understanding towards its treatment. I am a huge advocate for a d/Deaf and Hard of Hearing population. I am an American Sign Language (ASL) user. I am involved in the community and working on learning and advocating for better accessibility and visibility for the community. Every person deserves a fair and equal chance. No single person should be put to the side. The world needs to be a better and more inclusive place.

I am currently under supervision by Jennifer Agee, LCPC. If you have questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out to her at jennifer@jenniferagee.com

Have questions?

How can counseling help me?

What you will learn will help you become more comfortable with yourself. You will learn a variety techniques which can reconnect you to yourself and help you understand your inner self more deeply. We will work together to strengthen the connection with you. We will explore your body acceptance, learn about intuitive eating, explore mind body connection.

What is your treatment approach?

I use an eclectic approach. I use a combination of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Reality/Choice Therapy, and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy. In our first session we talk about what brought you into therapy and what has been bothering you. This way we can create a plan for what fits you and your goals.

How much do the counseling sessions cost?

Currently Private Pay only. I offer $75 - $125 per session and can talk to clients about their current financial situation. I also offer a superbill which you can give to your insurance company for reimbursement.

I accept American Express, Cash, Check, Discover, Mastercard, Venmo, and Visa

How can I contact you?

It's scary to start this journey, but I am here to create an open and judgement free space for you to explore yourself. I'm here for you. Contact me today through phone call, text, email, or booking an appointment on my website. I look forward to working with you on your journey!
