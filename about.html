Empowering Self-Discovery

Anna Carroll, LPC - Your Partner in Personal Growth.

Through a blend of cognitive behavioral therapy, acceptance commitment therapy, and holistic approaches, I empower clients to rediscover comfort within themselves and embrace their realities. From body acceptance to intuitive eating, we'll embark on a journey of self-discovery and healing, revitalizing the connection with your innermost self.

With expertise spanning addiction, anxiety, depression, and identity issues, I offer a safe space for exploration and growth. At Carroll Cares Counseling in Overland Park, KS, I provide individual counseling tailored to adolescents and adults, welcoming clients from all walks of life and backgrounds.

I look forward to working with you on your journey!
